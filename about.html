MY COLLEGE

College of Engineering Adoor is a private institute located in Pathanamthitta,Kerala. The institute was established in 1995 by IHRD and is affiliated to and recognized by APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and AICTE, New Delhi respectively. The campus is spread across a wide area of 12.7 acres and has excellent infrastructure facilities.

HIGHER SECONDARY

GOVT. BOYS HSS KOTTARAKKARA was established in 1894 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Urban area. It is located in KOTTARAKARA block of KOLLAM district of Kerala. The school consists of Grades from 8 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is Not Applicable in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Malayalam is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
